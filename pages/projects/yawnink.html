Project Summary

This website is a tattoo studio called YawnInk. It allows users to explore a wide variety of tattoo designs, view recent promotions and flashes, book appointments, and leave reviews. The website's key feature is a Pinterest-like gallery page, powered by MasonryJS, where users can browse through completed tattoos arranged in a dynamic, grid-style layout.

Additionally, users can easily book appointments with tattoo artists directly through the platform, selecting their preferred time and date. A review system lets customers share their experiences and rate their tattoos, building a trusted community of tattoo enthusiasts.

Rock Paper Scissors Game

Game Description

This is a simple yet engaging Rock Paper Scissors game, developed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It allows users to play against the computer by selecting one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The game provides real-time feedback on who won the round and tracks the score as the game progresses.
